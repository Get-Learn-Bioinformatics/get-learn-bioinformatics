Research Services

Our Services

Beyond training and mentorship, GLB offers hands-on computational research services for students, labs, and collaborators — built around our core specialty, Molecular Dynamics Simulation.

Flagship Service

Molecular Dynamics Simulation

We run GROMACS-based molecular dynamics simulations to study protein stability, conformational behavior, and protein–ligand interactions over time — the same pipeline used in our own published research. Configure the number of complexes, simulation length, and add-on analyses to match your project's needs.

  • Custom simulation time from 50 ns up to 1000 ns
  • Single or batch runs — up to 20 complexes per order
  • Optional PCA & DCCM post-simulation analysis
  • Optional molecular docking & ADMET add-ons
  • Full trajectory files, plots, and a written report delivered

Dynamic cross-correlation matrix mapping to reveal correlated and anti-correlated residue motions across the trajectory.

Add-on · per complex

How It Works

From Order to Results

A straightforward workflow so you always know where your analysis stands.

Featured Webinar

Explore AlphaFold

AlphaFold is a breakthrough AI tool developed by DeepMind that predicts the 3D structures of proteins from their amino acid sequences — transforming structural biology, drug design, protein engineering, and functional annotation.

Webinar hosted by Chinmoy Nandi & Mir Mohammod Ehsanul Haque.

Key Takeaways from the Webinar

✅ AlphaFold predicts protein structures with high accuracy from amino acid sequences alone.

✅ Directly applicable to drug discovery, molecular biology, and bioinformatics workflows.

✅ Free tools — AlphaFold DB and ColabFold — allow anyone to explore structure predictions.

✅ Understanding AlphaFold is key to the future of computational and structural biology.
